I gave a lecture at the [Oxford Machine Learning School - MLx Representation Learning & Generative AI 2025](https://www.oxfordml.school/2025) in August at Oxford.

The title of the talk was "A Journey of Tabular Benchmarks: Lessons in Method Comparison and Curation". In case you are interested, here are the [slides](https://geoalgo.github.io/downloads/Oxml-david-salinas.pdf?attredirects=0).

Thanks a lot to the organizer for inviting me! 

The end of the talk discusses and advocates for fully open-sourced LLMs and describes OpenEuroLLM, a recent European project on this.
