I am a Machine Learning Scientist working at Amazon.

My research focuses on machine-learning, in particular in probabilistic time-series forecasting and AutoML and hyperparameter optimization. In the past, I also worked on Computational geometry and topology and geometry processing.

I deeply enjoy taking inspiration of real-problems to do academic research (and the other way around too!). Some of this research went into AWS services such as the forecasting service DeepAR, SageMaker hyperparameter tuning service or more recently SageMaker inference recommender which allows to automatically select efficient endpoint configurations to deploy ML models.

I am also very keen on working on open-source, I am a core developer of Syne Tune (Hyperparameter Optimization), I was also a core-developer of Gluon-ts, Datawig and Gudhi.
